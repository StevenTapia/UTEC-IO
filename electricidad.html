Electricity and Magnetism

The course allows students to differentiate forms of obtaining electricity to explain the principles of the energy sources used by the system or equipment. Also, they acquire skills to analyze and measure electrical parameters of voltage, current and electrical resistance, relating through its fundamental laws Ohm and Kirchhoff, then determine the power, energy and power efficiency; With this knowledge they can measure and calculate the basic parameters of electricity.
