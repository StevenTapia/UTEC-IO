Differential Equations

The course allows students to use basic calculation tools to analyze and improve systems in technological areas such as electronics, control and telecommunications. Based on the knowledge that the student possesses differential and integral calculus, the course initially covers the fundamental concepts and methods for the solution of various types of differential equations of the first order.

Professor: Xyoby Chávez Pacheco
